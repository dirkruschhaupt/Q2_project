Friend Ranker
About

Are you always forgetting when and where you meet people? Do wish you could log friends and rate them by importance to your life? Well Friend Ranker is a personal app for keeping track of key information on friends, just the important stuff.

Store names, birthdays, images, where and when you meet people. Also store person information on each person incase you want to remember something on the person. Pull up the information quickly so you dont look like you have forgotten a person.
